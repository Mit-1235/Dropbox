Dropbox Replay Add-On

Get the premium features you need

With the Dropbox Replay Add-On, unlock access to the following features and more:

  • Upload as many files as your Dropbox plan’s storage allows
  • Assign deadlines, set passwords, and turn shared links on and off in a single place
  • View versions of your Replay files side-by-side and play back versions simultaneously
  • Deliver files up to 250 GB with Dropbox Transfer

Get the Dropbox Replay Add-On for $10 / month / user when billed annually (billed as $120).

* To purchase:

  • Basic users looking to purchase the Add-On will first need to upgrade to a paid plan. You’ll then be prompted to purchase the Replay Add-On.
  • On a Dropbox team? Reach out to your admin to ask about purchasing the Add-On.
  • If you’re an admin for a managed Standard, Advanced, Enterprise, Dropbox Business, or Dropbox Business Plus team and want to purchase the Add-On, reach out to your Account Manager.
  • The Add-On is not available for the Family plan.
