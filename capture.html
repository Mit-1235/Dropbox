What can you do with Capture?

Instead of scheduling a 30-minute meeting, send out a quick update with visuals for reference, and let everyone watch when they have time.
No more hard-to-follow bulleted lists—just record your screen as you walk through the steps, and show everyone how it’s done.
In place of time-consuming PDFs, emails, and in-person training, use Capture to create how-to videos and send them to anyone who needs support.
Pitch prospects with large teams without complicated scheduling, and give them a presentation they can pass along to other stakeholders.
Give clients the context they need by presenting layouts and explaining your thought process, so they get the complete picture.
Review class assignments with video feedback that they can easily digest—and you can easily create during late-night grading sessions.
